Meet Jeremy.

Cooper's Foodscaping was started in 2019 by Jeremy Cooper with the intention to bring the biodiversity and abundance of nature to your home. As a seasoned gardener, organic farmer, green roofer and landscaper, Jeremy has transformed dozens of yards into food forests, gardens, and edible landscapes.

He is especially fond of permaculture, using a variety of techniques that work within nature's patterns. Focusing on edible, native and non-invasive species — chemical-free, always.
